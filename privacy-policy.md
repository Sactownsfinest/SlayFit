# Privacy Policy for SlayFit

**Last updated: March 10, 2026**

## Overview

SlayFit ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the SlayFit mobile application.

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information**: Name, age, gender, height, weight, and fitness goals entered during onboarding
- **Health & fitness data**: Food logs, calorie intake, macronutrient tracking, workout sessions, activity logs, body measurements, and progress photos
- **User preferences**: Notification settings, dietary preferences, meal templates

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Step count and activity data**: Collected via your device's built-in pedometer sensor
- **Device information**: Device type and operating system version (for app compatibility)

### Third-Party Integrations (Optional)
- **Fitbit**: If you connect your Fitbit account, we access step count, calories burned, active minutes, and weight data via Fitbit's API. This requires your explicit authorization.
- **Google Health Connect**: If enabled, we read and write fitness and health data through Android's Health Connect platform.

---

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide personalized calorie targets, macro recommendations, and fitness plans
- Track your progress toward weight loss and fitness goals
- Generate AI-powered coaching advice and workout recommendations
- Send push notifications for reminders and goal updates (only if you enable them)
- Display your activity and nutrition history

---

## AI Coach

SlayFit includes an AI coaching feature powered by Groq's API. When you use the AI Coach, your fitness context (goals, recent activity, nutrition data) is sent to Groq's servers to generate responses. No personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.) is included in these requests. Please review [Groq's Privacy Policy](https://groq.com/privacy-policy/) for details on how they handle data.

---

## Data Storage

All your health and fitness data is stored **locally on your device** using Android's secure shared preferences storage. We do not operate servers that store your personal health data.

If you use the Community features (powered by Firebase), your display activity in challenges and leaderboards is stored in Firebase. Please review [Google's Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) for details.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We only share data with:
- **Fitbit** (if you connect your account) — governed by Fitbit's privacy policy
- **Groq** (AI Coach queries only, no personal identifiers)
- **Firebase/Google** (Community features only, if used)

---

## Health Data

SlayFit accesses Android Health Connect data solely to display your fitness metrics within the app. We do not share Health Connect data with third parties or use it for advertising purposes.

---

## Children's Privacy

SlayFit is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## Your Rights

You can:
- Delete all your data at any time from Profile → Settings → Delete Account
- Disconnect Fitbit at any time from Profile → Connected Apps
- Disable notifications at any time from Profile → Notifications

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
